About BPA

Business Professionals of America (BPA) is a student organization that provides students the opportunity to build professional skills through various competitions. “The Mission of Business Professionals of America is to contribute to the preparation of global professionals through the advancement of leadership, citizenship, academic, and technological skills.” BPA provides students with the resources to grow their leadership skills and creates competition to promote innovation.
